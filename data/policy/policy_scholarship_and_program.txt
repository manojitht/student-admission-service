UNIVERSITY ADMISSIONS & SCHOLARSHIP POLICY (v1.0)

Programs:
1) MSc Computer Science (MSCS)
   - Minimum overall GPA: 3.20/4.00
   - Must have at least 2 of the following:
     a) Data Structures & Algorithms grade >= B
     b) Database Systems grade >= B
     c) Operating Systems grade >= B
     d) Machine Learning or AI grade >= B
   - English proficiency: IELTS >= 6.5 OR TOEFL iBT >= 90 (if applicable)

2) MSc Artificial Intelligence (MSAI)
   - Minimum overall GPA: 3.40/4.00
   - Must have at least 2 of the following:
     a) Machine Learning grade >= B+
     b) Linear Algebra grade >= B
     c) Probability & Statistics grade >= B
     d) Deep Learning or Neural Networks grade >= B
   - Preferred: research experience or substantial project work in AI/ML.

Scholarship: "Merit Scholarship"
- Eligibility:
  - GPA >= 3.60/4.00
  - At least 1 strong recommendation letter explicitly stating:
      "top 10%" OR "exceptional" OR "outstanding"
  - No academic integrity violations
- Selection preference:
  - Evidence of leadership or impact
  - Publications or strong portfolio projects

Notes:
- GPA is on a 4.00 scale.
- Grade scale: A, A-, B+, B, B-, C+, C, D, F.
- For program requirements, grades are evaluated from transcript.
